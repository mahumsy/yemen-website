YEMEN IS IN NEED OF YOUR HELP
Yemen has been suffering through the worst humanitarian crisis in the world, on top of a civil war and COVID-19.

24 million people are in need of assistance.

That's around 80% of their total population.

This conflict began to escalate in 2015. That's 5 whole years of suffering.

Lack of clean water, food, or enough health facilities has caused many children to suffer from malnutrition and other illnesses like cholera.
